Air Quality Research Study

The purpose of this project is to provide you with information that will help you make decisions about local air quality. We want to see if providing you with air quality information will change your behaviour.

There are no known risks to getting this information but there may be a benefit to your health decisions.

The data that you enter into this survey is completely anonymous and cannot ever be linked to you individually. Because the data is stored on a US-based service provider database, your data could be subject to production orders under the USA Patriot Act.

You can quit this survey at any time. If you want to find out about this study in the future, we will post our results on www.crosh.ca.
